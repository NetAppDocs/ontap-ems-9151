== volaggr.offline
*Severity*::
ALERT
*Description*::
Some aggregates are forced offline. volume creation could cause duplicate FSIDs.
*Corrective Action*::
Bring all offline aggregates online.
*Syslog Message*::
Some aggregates are offline. Volume creation could cause duplicate FSIDs.
*Parameters*::
(None).
